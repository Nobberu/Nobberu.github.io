Texture Packs

Various Res

People sometimes like highly detailed textures to completely change how the game looks, sometimes they like the lower resolution textures to keep the original feeling of the game. We know how it feels and we want to provide our textures for everyone, so we made our textures in various resolution to match everyone's taste and/or everyone's device engine capability. You can chose 32x-64x for best performance, 128x-256x for balanced performance-quality ratio, or even 512x-1028x for the best quality. The choice is yours.

Physically Based

We provide Minecraft textures with Physically based standard materials such as normal maps and specular maps. Using the latest version of LabPBR (LabPBR 1.3) used by many shaders as of now. That being said, this feature requires a physically based supported shader to enable the advanced materials. You can try to install LabPBR 1.3 supported shader listed here.
